Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) are transforming the way computers interact with the world by enabling systems to learn from data and make intelligent decisions.
AI is a broad field focused on creating machines that can mimic human intelligence, including reasoning, problem-solving, and understanding language. 
ML, a subset of AI, specifically deals with algorithms that improve automatically through experience without being explicitly programmed. 
From personalized recommendations and virtual assistants to healthcare diagnostics and autonomous vehicles, these technologies are driving innovation across industries. 
As data continues to grow, AI and ML are becoming increasingly essential for uncovering insights, improving efficiency, and building smarter, more adaptive systems.
